About Rafael.

Born and raised in Panama City, Republic of Panama, Rafael is currently a first-year student at UC Berkeley, aiming to graduate with a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science and work alongside the government and several non-profit organizations to reduce the technological gap in less economically developed countries.

Rafael is a young amateur photographer that dreams with preserving and extending the presence of indigenous culture, language, and traditions in Latin America. Currently he is working in "Darien: A Project by Rafael Felix", which attempts to accurately depict the everyday life of the Embera Wounnan, one of the largest indigenous groups that still exist in Panama.

The purpose of this project is to transcend the mission of protecting our indigenous heritage, not only in Panama and Latin America, but across all countries and borders.
